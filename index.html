Introduction

What is Simply Sleep?

Hi, we are Simply Sleep - a student targeted sleep and habit tracker that not only tracks sleep, but enables tracking of sleep affecting habits such as caffeine and exercise.

Unlike other competing apps, Simply Sleep will then offer recommendations for how you can achieve a routine fit for you.

Discover what affects your sleep - from weather to screentime - and receive advice tailored for you.

With numerous automated and manual tracking features, finding your ideal routine has never been easier!

SWOT

For our SWOT competitor analysis, I researched 4 different highly rated sleep apps
Two were highly data driven, one assisted sleep with white noise and sleep stories, the fourth was a screen filter app
The two data driven apps left the user to figure out their own plan and locked the most important features behind paywalls
The sleep assister and screen filter apps helped with one aspect, but not the others

Group Stage

From ideation to interviews

The groupwork stage consisted of 6 main stages.

I recorded our group's progress in a separate blog:

Falcon UX Blog

The first stage was ideation, whereby a padlet was created with the intent to brainstorm a series of user groups and issues that we thought had space for a new app.

Following this, we chose the two issues that we felt were the most promising and developed elevator pitches. Of the two, one was then chosen to move forward with.

With our pitch in mind, we interviewed participants from a previous sample group to acquire user needs.

Next we drew common themes between our interviews and created three different personas, one for each area we felt the app should be able to assist with.

After that, we outlined what the app should accomplish and how through user journey maps.

Finally, we performed SWOT analysis on several popular apps that our app would be contending with in order to better understand the market space.
