Introduction

What is Simply Sleep?

Hi, we are Simply Sleep - a student targeted sleep and habit tracker that not only tracks sleep, but enables tracking of sleep affecting habits such as caffeine and exercise.

Unlike other competing apps, Simply Sleep will then offer recommendations for how you can achieve a routine fit for you.

Discover what affects your sleep - from weather to screentime - and receive advice tailored for you.

With numerous automated and manual tracking features, finding your ideal routine has never been easier!
